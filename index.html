Yamaha, Acoustic Guitar Fx310 /natural

Unbeatable value has made the dreadnought-style F310 a best seller. The perfect choice for discerning beginners, and also available as an electro-acoustic. The lively sound from the spruce/meranti body has a bright, sweet resonance not unlike Yamaha's renowned FGs, and real rosewood is used for the fingerboard and bridge. The handling comfort of the slightly reduced body depth and intermediate scale length will be particularly appreciated by younger players.
